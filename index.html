Our Curriculum

At Violets' Orchids Preschool, we follow a comprehensive curriculum that emphasizes experiential learning, ensuring that children not only learn but also apply their knowledge in real-life situations. Our curriculum is designed to foster holistic development, preparing children for future academic challenges.

Personal, Social, and Emotional Development

We focus on building confidence, independence, and social skills, helping children interact positively with peers and adults.

Communication, Language, and Literacy

Our programs enhance listening, speaking, reading, and writing abilities, laying the foundation for effective communication.

Mathematical Knowledge and Understanding

Through hands-on activities, children develop a strong grasp of basic mathematical concepts, fostering logical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Creative Development

We encourage creativity through art, music, and imaginative play, allowing children to express themselves and think innovatively.

Physical Development

Our curriculum includes activities that promote physical health, coordination, and motor skills, ensuring children develop a healthy lifestyle.
